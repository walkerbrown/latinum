LATINVM

A predictive text Latin keyboard for iOS, with word completion and next-word prediction powered by classical Latin texts.

✨ Features

  • Word completion — frequency-ranked predictions as you type
  • Next word prediction — based on context to keep your Latin flowing
  • Macrons & ligatures — long-press for easy access to ā ē ī ō ū, æ, œ
  • 100% offline — every keystroke stays on your device
