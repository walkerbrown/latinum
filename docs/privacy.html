Privacy Policy 🔒

Effective date: February 6, 2026

Latinum is designed to respect your privacy completely. Here's the short version: we don't collect anything.

Data Collection

Latinum collects no data — not usage data, not analytics, not keystrokes, not personal information. Nothing.

No Network Access

Latinum does not request the iOS "Full Access" keyboard entitlement. This means the operating system itself prevents the keyboard from accessing the network, the clipboard, or any other app's data. All word completion and prediction runs in a keyboard extension sandbox entirely on your device.

No Third-Party Services

There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no ad networks, no telemetry that phones home. Zero.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update it here with a new effective date.

Open Source

Latinum is open source! Feel free to audit the code repository.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@latinumkeyboard.org.
